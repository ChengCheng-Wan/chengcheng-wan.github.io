Resume       Google scholar       LinkedIn

Chengcheng Wan is a Postdoctoral Scholar at The University of Chicago. Previously, she received her Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from UChicago (2017-2022), advised by Professor Shan Lu. Her undergraduate studies were undertaken at Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (2013-2017).

Her research focus on improving machine learning software systems. She is also generally interested in SE/Sys for AI and AI for SE/Sys.
