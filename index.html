Software Engineering Institute, East China Normal University (ECNU), China
Email: ccwan at sei · ecnu · edu · cn       ORCID       Google scholar      

Chengcheng Wan is a tenure-track associate professor at East China Normal University. Prior to that, she was a Postdoctoral Scholar at The University of Chicago (2022-2023). She received her Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from UChicago (2017-2022), advised by Professor Shan Lu. Her undergraduate studies were undertaken at the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (2013-2017).

Her research focus on improving machine learning software systems. She is also generally interested in SE/Sys for AI and AI for SE/Sys.
